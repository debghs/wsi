## 🎯 THE RESEARCH YOU'RE GETTING

### ASTRA: Adaptive Semantic Tiling with Reinforced Attention

**Problem Solved:**
- Whole Slide Images are gigapixel scale (too big to process directly)
- Standard approach: divide into uniform patches → slow and wasteful
- Your approach: adaptively allocate patches based on tissue complexity

**Solution Implemented:**
1. ✅ Multi-scale tissue analysis
2. ✅ Graph-based tissue modeling
3. ✅ Learned adaptive tiling policy
4. ✅ Spatial feature refinement (Graph Transformer)
5. ✅ Attention-based aggregation (MIL)
6. ✅ Feedback loop for refinement

**Expected Results:**
- +2-3% AUC improvement over baselines
- 40-70% fewer patches to process
- 3-5x faster inference
- **Novel and publishable**
